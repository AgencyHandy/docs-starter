# Forms

In AgencyHandy, we've streamlined the process of gathering client information and facilitating payments with our innovative Form Setup feature. With this tool, users can effortlessly create two distinct types of forms: Order forms and Intake forms.

### Why is it essential?&#x20;

Imagine a world where your clients can seamlessly select services and make payments all in one go. That's precisely what our Order forms offer. By allowing clients to choose multiple services and complete transactions on a single page, we've made the process smoother and more convenient than ever before.

But we didn't stop there. Our Intake forms take client interactions to the next level by allowing you to delve deeper into their requirements. Say goodbye to endless back-and-forth emails trying to gather detailed information. With Intake forms, you can capture all the necessary details upfront, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of each order.

### Advantages:

\* \*\*Convenience:\*\* Clients can easily access and complete forms using unique links, saving time and effort for both parties.&#x20;
\* \*\*Flexibility:\*\* Order forms empower clients to select and pay for multiple services simultaneously, enhancing their overall experience.&#x20;
\* \*\*Customization:\*\* Intake forms enable tailored information collection, ensuring you have all the details needed to deliver exceptional service.&#x20;

With Form Setup in AgencyHandy, simplify client interactions and streamline your workflow like never before.
