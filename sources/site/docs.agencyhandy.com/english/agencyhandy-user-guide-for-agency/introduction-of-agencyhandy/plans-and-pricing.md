AgencyHandy currently offers three plans, with prices available for annual, **monthly, and LTD**. You can save 30% in monthly, 63% in yearly, and whooping 71% in the LTD plan.

The plans are:

- **Freelancer**
 
- **Team Starter**
 
- **Business Pro**
 

### 

The plans and prices are:

### 

**Freelancer**

Best fit for freelancers & solopreneurs

**$19/monthly ( 31% off) $129/yearly ( 63% off) $199/LTD ( 71% off)**

[Choose Freelancer](https://www.agencyhandy.com/new-pricing-plan/)

This plan includes

- 1 user
 
- Single Workspace
 
- Up to 100 leads
 
- 1 GB file upload
 
- Storage up to 100 GB
 
- Unlimited clients
 
- Unlimited orders
 
- CRM (Leads & Clients)
 
- Custom Forms
 
- Task Management
 
- Time Tracking
 
- Invoicing & Subscription
 
- Notes
 
- Client and Internal Team Conversation
 
- Client Approval
 
- File Feedback
 

### 

**Team Starter**

Perfect for small and medium agencies

**$69/monthly ( 31% off) $439/yearly ( 63% off) $699/LTD ( 71% off)**

[Choose Team Starter](https://www.agencyhandy.com/new-pricing-plan/)

This plan includes everything in Freelancers, plus:

- Up to 10 members
 
- Single Workspace
 
- Up to 3,000 leads
 
- 1 Custom Email
 
- 2 GB File Upload
 
- Storage up to 2 TB
 
- Slack Integration
 
- Custom Domain
 
- White Labeling
 

### 

**Business Pro**

Most popular amongst larger agencies

**$139/monthly ( 31% off) $889/yearly ( 63% off) $1399/LTD ( 71% off)**

[Choose Business Pro](https://www.agencyhandy.com/new-pricing-plan/)

This plan includes everything in Team Starter, plus:

- Up to 30 members
 
- Up to 5 Workspaces
 
- Up to 10,000 Leads
 
- 5 Custom Email
 
- 10 GB File Upload
 
- Storage up to 10 TB
 
- 30,000 Webhook Events
