# Plans & Pricing

AgencyHandy currently offers three plans, with prices available for annual, \*\*monthly, and LTD\*\*. You can save 30% in monthly, 63% in yearly, and whooping 71% in the LTD plan.&#x20;

The plans are:

\* \*\*Freelancer\*\*&#x20;
\* \*\*Team Starter\*\*
\* \*\*Business Pro\*\*

### The plans and prices are:

### \*\*Freelancer\*\*&#x20;

Best fit for freelancers & solopreneurs

\*\*$19/monthly ( 31% off)\*\* \\
\*\*$129/yearly ( 63% off)\*\* \\
\*\*$199/LTD ( 71% off)\*\*&#x20;

\[Choose Freelancer\](https://www.agencyhandy.com/new-pricing-plan/)

This plan includes

\* 1 user&#x20;
\* Single Workspace
\* Up to 100 leads&#x20;
\* 1 GB file upload
\* Storage up to 100 GB&#x20;
\* Unlimited clients&#x20;
\* Unlimited orders&#x20;
\* CRM (Leads & Clients)
\* Custom Forms
\* Task Management&#x20;
\* Time Tracking
\* Invoicing & Subscription&#x20;
\* Notes
\* Client and Internal Team Conversation&#x20;
\* Client Approval
\* File Feedback

### \*\*Team Starter\*\*&#x20;

Perfect for small and medium agencies

\*\*$69/monthly ( 31% off)\*\* \\
\*\*$439/yearly ( 63% off)\*\* \\
\*\*$699/LTD ( 71% off)\*\*&#x20;

\[Choose Team Starter\](https://www.agencyhandy.com/new-pricing-plan/)

This plan includes everything in Freelancers, plus:

\* Up to 10 members&#x20;
\* Single Workspace
\* Up to 3,000 leads
\* 1 Custom Email&#x20;
\* 2 GB File Upload
\* Storage up to 2 TB&#x20;
\* Slack Integration&#x20;
\* Custom Domain
\* White Labeling

### \*\*Business Pro\*\*

Most popular amongst larger agencies

\*\*$139/monthly ( 31% off)\*\* \\
\*\*$889/yearly ( 63% off)\*\* \\
\*\*$1399/LTD ( 71% off)\*\*&#x20;

\[Choose Business Pro\](https://www.agencyhandy.com/new-pricing-plan/)

This plan includes everything in Team Starter, plus:

\* Up to 30 members&#x20;
\* Up to 5 Workspaces
\* Up to 10,000 Leads&#x20;
\* 5 Custom Email
\* 10 GB File Upload
\* Storage up to 10 TB&#x20;
\* 30,000 Webhook Events
