# Invoices

With AgencyHandy's invoice feature, businesses can manage their invoicing and billing processes efficiently. This comprehensive solution makes it easier to keep track of financial transactions and get paid. The invoice feature of AgencyHadny's platform manages and facilitates billing and invoicing processes.

<figure><img src="/files/ofNVCuh21JqoBxhY5vmt" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Here's a comprehensive overview of what the invoice feature does:

1. &#x20;\*\*Billing and Invoicing\*\*: With AgencyHandy, users can easily create and send invoices for services or products sold to clients or customers.
2. &#x20;\*\*Payment Handling\*\*: Clients can make payments directly through AgencyHandy's platform in response to invoices, streamlining the payment process and improving efficiency.
3. &#x20;\*\*Integration with Project Management\*\*: AgencyHandy's invoice feature can integrate with project management tools. It allows users to track time spent on specific projects or tasks and add billable items to invoices, accurately billing clients.
4. &#x20;\*\*Recurring Billing\*\*: The platform enables automated generation and delivery of invoices for businesses offering subscription-based services.
5. &#x20;\*\*Online Accessibility\*\*: AgencyHandy's invoicing is web-based (SaaS), meaning users can access it from any device with a web browser and an internet connection.
