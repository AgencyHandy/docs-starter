Proposal Settings allow you to customize the appearance, numbering, visibility, and reminder behavior of proposals. These settings control how proposals look to clients and how they are structured internally, ensuring consistency with your agency’s branding and workflow.

---

### 

What You Can Do

- Define a custom proposal number format
 
- Upload and display your agency logo
 
- Customize background, theme, and text colors
 
- Hide specific proposal sections from client view
 
- Add an agency signature
 
- Configure proposal reminder behavior
 
- Preview all changes in real time before saving
 

---

### 

Why It Matters

- Maintains consistent branding across all proposals
 
- Improves proposal readability and professionalism
 
- Gives agencies control over what clients see
 
- Reduces manual formatting work for each proposal
 
- Ensures proposal IDs follow internal standards
 

---

### 

Who Can Use This

- Super Admin
 
- Admin
 

---

### 

Accessing Proposal Settings

Navigate to:

**left Column → Proposals → Settings (at the top right corner)**

This opens the Proposal Settings page, with a live preview on the right.

---

### 

Proposal Number Format

#### 

Proposal Prefix

- Set a custom prefix for proposal IDs (e.g., `PRO-`)
 
- The system automatically appends dates or numbers after the prefix
 
- Helps maintain consistent internal tracking and identification
 

---

### 

Style: Text & Logo

#### 

Logo

- Upload your agency logo (recommended size: 32×32)
 
- The logo appears at the top of every proposal
 
- Optional — proposals can be sent without a logo
 

---

### 

Style: Color

#### 

Background Color

- Sets the overall background color of the proposal
 
- Default is white (`#ffffff`)
 

#### 

Theme Color

- Controls accent elements such as borders, dividers, and headings
 
- Used consistently across the proposal layout
 

#### 

Text Color

- Defines the primary text color for content sections
 
- Ensures readability and brand alignment
 

---

### 

Hide Content

You can choose to hide specific sections from client-facing proposals:

- Agency Signature
 
- Terms & Conditions
 
- Client Address
 
- About Us
 
- Tax Rates
 
- Company Name
 

Hidden sections will not appear in the proposal preview or public view.

---

### 

Agency Signature

- Add a custom agency signature
 
- Signature appears at the bottom of proposals
 
- Useful for branding, legal acknowledgment, or sign-off text
 

---

### 

Proposal Reminder Settings

- Configure automated reminders for pending proposals
 
- Helps follow up with clients who haven’t responded
 
- Reminder rules can be edited or disabled as needed
 

---

### 

Live Preview

- All changes are reflected instantly in the preview panel
 
- Shows how the proposal will appear to clients
 
- Ensures accuracy before saving changes
 

---

### 

Saving Changes

- Click **Save** to apply all settings
 
- Click **Cancel** to discard changes
 
- Saved settings apply to all new proposals going forward

### 

Additional Notes

- Proposal Settings apply globally across the workspace
 
- Existing proposals remain unchanged unless edited
 
- Colors and hidden sections affect both the preview and the public view
 
- Proposal numbering follows the defined prefix automatically
