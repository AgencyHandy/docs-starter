The Resetting Password feature in AgencyHandy enables users to regain access to their accounts in case of a forgotten password. It provides a secure and straightforward process for users to reset their passwords, ensuring seamless access to the platform.

## 

**Steps to Use:**

- **Access Reset Password Option:** Go to the AgencyHandy login page.
 
- **Initiate Password Reset:** **Click** the **Forgot Password** link on the login page.
 
- **Enter Email:** Input the email associated with your AgencyHandy account.
 

![](https://docs.agencyhandy.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F842362573-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FiedbxyF6rrnGBuHuFME1%252Fuploads%252Fg7YuFO7Id8uf6v8YiOnT%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D136780ac-c15a-4908-99fd-c8947da4e306&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=79ee925d&sv=2)

- **Email Verification:** Check your email for a verification code from AgencyHandy.
 
- **Verification Code:** Copy the verification code and paste it.
 

![](https://docs.agencyhandy.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F842362573-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FiedbxyF6rrnGBuHuFME1%252Fuploads%252FjLTdx2uhLdZCbzYNCWA8%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D5cf6fb07-b19f-44bd-9a76-7d0eaaf9b554&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=7d853f0b&sv=2)

- **Reset Password:** Click on the link in the email to access the password reset page.
 
- **Create a New Password:** Enter and confirm your new password.
 

![](https://docs.agencyhandy.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F842362573-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FiedbxyF6rrnGBuHuFME1%252Fuploads%252FxLL2pt6aklTgzOxHQXvL%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Ddec469c3-59c5-486d-851a-9e57c0e01c5c&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=3d225c3d&sv=2)

- **Login:** Return to the login page and enter with your new password.
 

Follow these steps to reset your password and regain access to your AgencyHandy account effortlessly.
