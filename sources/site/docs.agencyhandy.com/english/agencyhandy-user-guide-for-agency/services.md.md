# Services

{% embed url="<https://youtu.be/q0qiIHWpnBc>" %}

The "Service" feature in AgencyHandy simplifies service management, enabling you to create, describe, and price your services comprehensively. The ability to upload cover images, set pricing structures, and apply filters and sorting makes it a powerful tool for effectively showcasing and selling your agency's services.
