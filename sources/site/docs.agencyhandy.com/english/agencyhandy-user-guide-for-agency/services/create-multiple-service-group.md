The "Create Multiple Service Group" feature allows users to organize multiple services into a single group. This is useful for managing and presenting related services, enhancing user experience, and managing services.

### 

**Benefits**

- **Enhanced Organization:** Group-related services for better management and visibility.
 
- **Improved Presentation:** Present services as a cohesive package to clients, making it easier for them to understand and choose relevant offerings.
 
- **Streamlined Access:** Simplify the process of accessing and managing multiple services by grouping them.
 
- **Increased Flexibility:** Allow better control over how services are displayed and grouped in your catalog.
 

### 

**Pre-conditions**

- **User Permissions:** Ensure you have administrative or appropriate permissions to create and manage service groups.
 
- **Existing Services:** Have multiple services ready to be grouped together.
 
- **Catalogue Access:** Ensure you have access to the service catalogue where you want to create the group.
 

### 

**Steps to Use**

1. **Log in to Your Account**
 
 - Access your Agency Handy account using your credentials.
 
 
2. **Navigate to the Services Section**
 
 - Go to the "Services" section from the main menu to view all available services. Click the "Select Multiple Service" button.

3. **Select Multiple Services**
 
 - Choose the services you want to group by selecting the checkboxes next to them.
 
 
 ![](https://docs.agencyhandy.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F842362573-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FiedbxyF6rrnGBuHuFME1%252Fuploads%252FdYFw73aNklYOsJsHPR8i%252FScreenshot_245.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D7bd1c039-a47f-4fc5-b394-3616b89fb402&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=e42dc288&sv=2)
 
4. **Group Services**
 
 - Click the "Group Selected Service" button to open a new form for creating a package group.
 
 
 ![](https://docs.agencyhandy.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F842362573-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FiedbxyF6rrnGBuHuFME1%252Fuploads%252FFSLJ7JZP9DaavcNNQ96n%252FScreenshot_246.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dc4806de1-4330-4e49-828a-c9a303b7ac2c&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=aacf4a23&sv=2)
 
5. **Fill Out the Group Form**
 
 - **Group Name:** Enter a name for the group that reflects the services included (e.g., "Marketing Essentials Bundle").
 
 - **Group Description:** Provide a brief description outlining the group’s offerings and purpose.
 
 - **Upload Group Images:** Add images to represent the group visually.
 
 - **Publish Options:** Choose whether to publish the group immediately or later.
 
 - **Catalogue Visibility:** Select "Do Not Show to Catalog" if you do not want the group to be visible in the catalogue.
 
 - Click the "Save" button to finalize the creation of the package group.

6. **View Group**
 
 - After saving, click the "View Group Service" button to view the grouped services. From this view, you can review and manage the services within the group.

## 

Important Notes

- **Manual Orders:** When creating manual orders, select the client first to apply any visibility restrictions before selecting services.
 
- **Unique Group Assignments:** Each service can belong to only one group at a time.
 
- **Design Limits:** Descriptions longer than 140 characters may disrupt group design formatting.
