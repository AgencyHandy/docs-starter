The Service Edit Layout feature in Agency Handy allows you to organize and control how your services appear to clients inside the Service Catalog. This ensures a cleaner browsing experience, highlights your priority offerings, and allows selective visibility for different service groups.

You can reorder services, publish or unpublish them, manage ratings visibility, and preview the catalog interface — giving you complete control over the client-facing marketplace experience.

---

### 

What You Can Do

With Edit Layout, you can:

- Reorder services using drag-and-drop
 
- Enable or disable visibility (Published / Unpublished)
 
- View service type, pricing, and performance insights such as ratings and comments
 
- Preview how the catalog looks to clients
 
- Show or hide service groups from the catalog view
 
- Restrict visibility of specific groups to selected client segments
 

---

### 

Who Can Use This

This feature is accessible for:

- Super Admin
 
- Admin
 
- Manager (based on permissions)
 

---

### 

Managing Your Service Layout

#### 

Rearrange Service Order

- Click and drag the **Drag Handle** beside each service
 
- Place services in the preferred sequence
 
- Higher priority or popular services can be moved to the top
 

This helps clients discover your most important services first.

---

#### 

Publish or Unpublish Services

Each service has a toggle in the **Published** column:

- Enable → Service becomes visible and available for purchase
 
- Disable → Service remains hidden from clients, but still editable internally
 

Useful for services that are being tested or temporarily unavailable.

---

#### 

Monitor Service Performance

From this screen, you can see:

- ⭐ Average rating
 
- 💬 Total number of reviews/comments
 

This helps you evaluate which services are performing best and where improvements are needed.

---

#### 

Preview the Catalog

You can check how the catalog appears on the client side:

- Click **Catalog Preview**
 
- Ensure visuals, order, and visibility are aligned with your brand strategy
 

This prevents layout issues before clients view it.

---

#### 

Show or Hide Service Groups

Use the **Show Groups** toggle to:

- Display grouped service categories (if grouping is configured)
 
- Present services individually without categorization
 

This gives you flexibility for both minimal and structured catalog layouts.

---

#### 

Restrict Group Visibility

Under the section title _“You can restrict specific clients from viewing particular group of Service”_

- Services in a restricted group will not appear for selected client teams or individuals
 
- Helps tailor offerings for different market segments
 

Example Use Cases:

- Enterprise-only solutions
 
- NDA-protected service offerings
 
- Tier-based service access

### 

Important Notes

- Service must be **Published** to be visible on the client catalog
 
- Reordering affects client visibility immediately — no additional save required
 
- Ratings and reviews are collected automatically from client feedback after order delivery
 
- If a service is hidden by unpublished status or group restriction, clients cannot search for it
