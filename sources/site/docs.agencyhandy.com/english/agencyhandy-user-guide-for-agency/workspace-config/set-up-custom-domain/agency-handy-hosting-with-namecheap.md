Agency Handy integrates with Namecheap to provide seamless hosting for your platform. This feature enables users to manage their hosting services directly through Agency Handy, streamlining the hosting setup and maintenance process. The Namecheap Hosting feature allows users to host their Agency Handy platform using Namecheap’s domain and hosting services.

### 

**Purpose:**

- **Streamlined Hosting:** Simplifies the hosting process by integrating with Namecheap, allowing direct management from Agency Handy.
 
- **Convenience:** Eliminates the need for external platforms and reduces complexity.
 
- **Efficiency:** Offers an easy way to manage hosting services using Agency Handy.
 

### 

**Pre-conditions:**

- An active **Agency Handy** account.
 
- An active **Namecheap** account with registered domains and hosting services.
 

---

### 

**Steps to Set Up Agency Handy Hosting with Namecheap (Detailed):**

1. **Access Hosting Settings:**
 
 - Log in to your Agency Handy account.
 
 - Navigate to the **Hosting Settings** section within the platform or account settings menu.
 
 
2. **Select Namecheap Integration:**
 
 - Locate the option for **Namecheap Integration** in the hosting settings.
 
 - Click on the Namechip integration option to proceed.
 
 
3. **Authorize Namecheap Access:**
 
 - Follow the on-screen prompts to authorize access to your Namecheap account.
 
 - This may involve logging into Namecheap and granting permission for the integration.
 
 
4. **Link Accounts:**
 
 - Provide your Namecheap account credentials or API key as required.
 
 - Link your Agency Handy account with your Namecheap hosting account.
 
 
5. **Configure Hosting Settings:**
 
 - After linking the accounts, configure the hosting settings as needed:
 
 - **Select Hosting Plan:** Choose the appropriate hosting plan offered by Namecheap.
 
 - **Specify Domain Settings:** Set up your domain details, such as linking your custom domain.
 
 - **Configure Security Options:** Enable SSL/TLS or other security features provided by Namecheap.
 
 - **Additional Features:** Set up any additional hosting features Namecheap offers.
 
 
 
6. **Review and Confirm:**
 
 - Double-check the configured hosting settings to ensure accuracy.
 
 - Make necessary adjustments if needed.
 
 
7. **Save Changes:**
 
 - Once satisfied with the setup, click the **Save Changes** button to apply the integration.
 
 - Your hosting is now configured with Namecheap for Agency Handy.
 
 

---

### 

**Important Notes:**

- Ensure your Namecheap account has active domain registration and hosting services before starting the integration.
 
- Linking your accounts requires proper authorization and may involve API key setup or credential verification.
 
- Review hosting settings carefully to avoid misconfigurations that could disrupt your platform.
 
- Enable Namecheap's security features, such as SSL/TLS, to enhance the security of your hosted platform.
