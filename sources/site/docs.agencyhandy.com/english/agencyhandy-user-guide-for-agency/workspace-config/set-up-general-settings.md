The **General Settings** feature in Agency Handy allows agencies to personalize their workspace by setting a logo, workspace name, address, and time zone. These configurations help represent the agency’s identity consistently across the platform.

#### 

Goals

- Establish clear brand identity with logo and workspace name.
 
- Ensure accurate representation of location and time zone.
 
- Enable seamless navigation and consistent user experience across time-sensitive features.
 

#### 

Pre-conditions

🛠️ **Permissions Required:** Only **Super Admins** and **Admins** can configure general workspace settings.

🌐 **Account Access:** You must be logged into your **Agency Handy** account with the appropriate role.

### 

1\. Accessing General Settings

1. Log in to your **Agency Handy** account.
 
2. From the left navigation menu, click on **Workspace Config**.
 
3. Select **General Settings**.
 

📌 _Only users with admin permissions will see this option._

---

### 

2\. Uploading Workspace Logos

You can upload three types of logos:

- **Circle Logo**: 32x32 pixels
 
- **Favicon Logo**: 16x16 pixels
 
- **Rectangle Logo**: 142x36 pixels
 

#### 

To Upload:

- Click on **Upload Logo**.
 
- Select the image from your device.
 
- Supported formats: **PNG, JPG, SVG**
 

💡 _Uploading a rectangle logo replaces the sidebar logo and hides the workspace name beside it._

---

### 

3\. Setting Workspace Name & Address

#### 

Workspace Name:

- Enter your agency’s name in the provided field.
 
- This name appears across the platform for identification.
 

#### 

Workspace Address:

- Input your complete address.
 
- Ensure accuracy for official representation.
 

---

### 

4\. Selecting a Time Zone

- Open the **Time Zone** dropdown.
 
- Select the zone that matches your agency’s location.
 
- This ensures all timestamps and schedules (e.g., meetings, tasks) are aligned.
 

⚠️ _Incorrect time zone settings may lead to scheduling errors._

---

### 

5\. Saving & Reviewing Changes

- Review all entered details.
 
- Click the **Save Changes** button to apply updates.
 

🎯 _Your workspace is now personalized with the updated general settings._

---

### 

6\. Danger Zone: Deleting Workspace

- Scroll to the **Danger Zone** section in the General Settings panel.
 
- Click **Delete Workspace** only if you're certain.
 

⚠️ _This action is irreversible. Deleting the workspace will permanently erase all data including clients, orders, invoices, and configurations._

---

### 

7\. Best Practices

- Regularly update logo and address details to reflect current branding and location.
 
- Only authorized personnel should make changes to workspace configurations.
 
- Verify logo dimensions before uploading to avoid formatting errors.
 

---

### 

📝 Important Notes

- The **circle**, **favicon**, and **rectangle** logo types serve different branding purposes and may appear in different sections of the platform.
 
- **Rectangle logos** will hide the workspace name from the sidebar when uploaded.
 
- The rectangle logo overtakes the cycle logo, meaning that when you upload it, it becomes the primary logo for all cases, despite the circle logo still being there.
 
- Make sure uploaded logo files meet the exact dimensions and format requirements for proper display.
 
- **Time zone selection** is critical for correct task deadlines, scheduled notifications, and calendar events.
 
- Changes to general settings are applied globally and affect all users within the workspace.
 
- Deleting a workspace cannot be undone and removes all associated data permanently.
 

---

🎯 _You’ve successfully configured General Settings in Agency Handy. Your workspace is now accurately represented and aligned for optimal platform experience._
