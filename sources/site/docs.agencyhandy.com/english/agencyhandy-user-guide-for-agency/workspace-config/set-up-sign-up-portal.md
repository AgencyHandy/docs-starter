is The Sign-up Portal feature in Agency Handy allows agencies to customize the appearance of their sign-up page to reflect their brand identity. Agencies can personalize the sign-up page by providing a custom heading, uploading images, and adding a brief description.

### 

Purpose:

Customizing the sign-up portal is essential for the following reasons:

- **Brand Consistency:** Tailoring the sign-up page to align with the agency's brand identity ensures consistency across all touchpoints, reinforcing brand recognition.
 
- **Professionalism:** A customized sign-up page gives a professional impression to users and clients, enhancing the overall user experience.
 
- **Brand Identity:** By incorporating branding elements such as logos and colors, agencies can strengthen their brand identity and create a memorable first impression for users.
 

### 

Pre-condition to Use the Feature:

- Users must have administrative access to the Agency Handy workspace to configure the sign-up portal settings.
 

### 

Steps to Use:

- **Access Workspace Settings:** Log in to Agency Handy and navigate to the workspace settings section.
 
- **Navigate to Sign-up Portal Settings:** Look for the option related to Sign-up Portal Settings within the workspace settings menu.
 
- **Customize Heading:** Enter a custom heading for the sign-up page in the provided field. The heading should be concise and limited to a maximum of 120 characters.
 
- **Upload Images:** Use the upload or drag-and-drop functionality to add images (png, jpg, jpeg) to the sign-up page. Ensure that the image size does not exceed 5 MB to maintain optimal performance.
 
- **Add Subtext:** Provide a short description or subtext for the sign-up page in the designated field. The subtext should be brief and limited to a maximum of 300 characters.
 
- **Preview and Save Changes:** Review the customized sign-in page layout using the preview option. Once satisfied with the changes, click on the "Save" or "Update" button to apply the modifications.
 

### 

Important Notes:

- **Purpose of the Sign-up Portal**
 
 - This feature allows users to **customize the appearance** of their sign-up page to align with their agency's branding.
 
 - A well-designed sign-up portal **enhances user experience** and improves brand recognition.
 
 - **Customizable Elements**
 
 - **Heading:**
 
 - Users can set a custom heading to display at the top of the sign-up page.
 
 - Maximum character limit: **120 characters**.
 
 
 - **Image Upload:**
 
 - Users can upload an image to personalize the sign-up page.
 
 - **Supported file types:** PNG, JPG, JPEG.
 
 - **Maximum file size:** 5MB.
 
 
 - **Subtext:**
 
 - A **short message** displayed below the login fields to provide guidance or branding.
 
 - Maximum character limit: **300 characters**.
 
 
 
 - **Access & Permissions**
 
 - Only **Super Admins and Admins** have permission to configure the Sign-up Portal settings.
 
 - Changes apply immediately upon saving.
 
 
 - **Reset to Default**
 
 - Clicking **"Reset Default Settings"** will restore the portal to its **original system settings**, removing all customizations.
 
 - This action **cannot be undone**, so proceed with caution.
 
 
 - **Saving Changes**
 
 - Once all customizations are made, users must click **"Save"** to apply changes.
 
 - Clicking **"Cancel"** will discard any unsaved modifications.
 
 
 - **Troubleshooting & Best Practices**
 
 - If the **image fails to upload**, ensure it meets the **format and size requirements**.
 
 - Keep the **heading and subtext concise** to maintain a clean and professional appearance.
 
 - Preview changes before saving to ensure the **branding is correctly displayed**.
