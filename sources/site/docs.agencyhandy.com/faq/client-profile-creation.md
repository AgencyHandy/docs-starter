**AgencyHandy Client Profile Creation – Frequently Asked Questions**

---

**1\. What is the Client Profile feature in AgencyHandy?**

The Client Profile feature allows users to add or invite clients to the platform, enabling centralized communication, project management, and collaboration.

---

**2\. Who can create a client profile in AgencyHandy?**

Only Super Admins or Admins with active accounts on AgencyHandy can create or invite clients.

---

**3\. What information is required to create a client profile?**

You will need to provide the client’s name, email address, and any other relevant details.

---

**4\. How do I access the client creation feature?**

Log in to your AgencyHandy account, navigate to the **Clients** section, and click on **\+ Create Client** at the top right corner.

---

**5\. Can I edit a client’s profile after it is created?**

Yes, Admins and Super Admins can update client details anytime from the **Clients** section.

---

**6\. Will the client receive a notification after profile creation?**

Yes, the client will receive an email containing a secure login URL.

---

**7\. What happens when the client clicks the secure login URL?**

Clicking the URL will create the client profile and prompt the client to set up their access.

---

**8\. Can I create a client profile without an email address?**

No, the email address is mandatory to send the login URL and complete the client profile setup.

---

**9\. What should I do if the client did not receive the login URL email?**

Check for any typos in the email address or ask the client to check their spam folder. You can also resend the invitation from the client profile.

---

**10\. Can I invite multiple clients at once?**

Currently, client profiles are created one by one. You need to repeat the process for each client.

---

**11\. How do I verify if the client profile is successfully created?**

Once created, the client profile will appear in the **Clients** list in your dashboard.

---

**12\. Can I delete a client profile after creation?**

Yes, Admins can delete client profiles if they are no longer needed.

---

**13\. Will deleting a client profile remove their data from the system?**

Yes, deleting a profile permanently removes the client’s data and cannot be undone. However, if that client has ongoing orders, they will grey out and become cancelled.

---

**14\. Can clients update their own profile information?**

No. They can't.

---

**15\. Is it possible to restrict a client’s access to certain features?**

No. Right now, Agency Handy doesn't have any role edit preference.

---

**16\. Can I resend the secure login URL to a client if needed?**

Yes, you can resend the invitation from the client section.

---

**17\. What should I do if the client’s email address is incorrect?**

Delete the client profile and create it again with the updated email address, then resend the login invitation.

---

**18\. Can I see whether the client has activated their profile?**

Yes, the client profile will show an **Active** status once the client completes the setup.

---

**19\. What if the client forgets their login credentials?**

Clients can use the **Forgot Password** option on the login page to reset their password.

---

**20\. How do I manage client profiles after creation?**

You can manage client profiles from the Clients section, where you can see the clients' recent orders, tickets, invoices, notes, and edit or delete profiles as needed.

---

**21\. Can I export a list of all client profiles?**

Yes, you can export client data from the **Clients** section in CSV or Excel format.

---

**22\. Is there a limit to the number of client profiles I can create?**

No. There is no limit to the number of client profiles right now.

---

**23\. Can I assign multiple admins to manage client profiles?**

Yes, you can grant admin privileges to other users to manage client profiles.

---

**24\. What should I do if I accidentally delete a client profile?**

Once deleted, client profiles cannot be recovered. Always double-check before confirming deletion.

---

**25\. Can I customize the email template sent to clients during profile creation?**

Yes, you can customize the welcome email from the **Email Template** settings in your dashboard.
