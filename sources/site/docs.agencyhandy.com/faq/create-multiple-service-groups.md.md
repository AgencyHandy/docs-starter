# Create Multiple Service Groups

## FAQ: Create Multiple Service Groups in AgencyHandy

#### 1. What is the purpose of creating a multiple service group?

Creating a multiple service group allows you to organize related services under a single group, enhancing presentation and management.

#### 2. Can I include services from different categories into one group?

Yes, you can group services from different categories into a single service group.

#### 3. Are there any prerequisites for creating a multiple service group?

Yes, you must have administrative permissions and existing services to group.

#### 4. How many services can I include in a single group?

There is no fixed limit, but it is recommended to group services logically for better client understanding.

#### 5. Can a single service belong to multiple groups?

No, each service can be assigned to only one group at a time.

#### 6. Can I rename an existing service group after creating it?

Yes, you can edit the group name later by accessing the group settings.

#### 7. How can I make a group invisible to clients?

During group creation, select the 'Do Not Show to Catalog' option to keep it hidden from clients.

#### 8. Is it possible to delete a service group?

Yes, you can delete a group by accessing the group settings and selecting the delete option.

#### 9. Can I add images to the service group?

Yes, you can upload group images to enhance visual appeal during the group creation process.

#### 10. How do I manage individual services within a group?

Go to the group view and select the specific service you want to manage or edit.

#### 11. Can I add a description to the service group?

Yes, you can provide a brief description to explain the purpose and offerings of the group.

#### 12. What happens if I ungroup a service from a group?

The service will revert to being listed individually and no longer appear within the group.

#### 13. Is there a limit on the length of the group description?

Yes, the group description should not exceed 140 characters to maintain proper formatting.

#### 14. How do I publish the service group immediately after creation?

Select the 'Publish' option during the group creation process to make it live instantly.

#### 15. Can I save a draft of the group without publishing it?

Yes, you can choose not to publish immediately and save it as a draft instead.

#### 16. Will clients see individual services when they view the group?

Yes, clients will see the list of individual services included in the group when they access it.

#### 17. Can I update the group settings after it has been created?

Yes, you can update group details, images, and visibility settings anytime.

#### 18. How do I view all grouped services together?

Go to the 'Services' section and select 'View Group Service' to see all the grouped services.

#### 19. Can I duplicate a group to create a similar one?

Currently, AgencyHandy does not support duplicating a service group. You need to create a new group manually.

#### 20. What happens if I remove a service from the group?

The service will be listed individually again and removed from the group structure.

#### 21. How can I ensure the group displays correctly on the client portal?

Ensure that descriptions are concise and images meet the recommended size to avoid formatting issues.

#### 22. Can I archive a group instead of deleting it?

Currently, there is no option to archive; you can either keep the group active or delete it.

#### 23. What if I accidentally delete a service group?

Once deleted, the group cannot be recovered. You will need to recreate it.

#### 24. Can I track the usage or views of the service group?

Currently, AgencyHandy does not provide analytics specifically for grouped services.

#### 25. Are group changes reflected immediately on the client portal?

Yes, any updates to the group are reflected in real-time once saved.
