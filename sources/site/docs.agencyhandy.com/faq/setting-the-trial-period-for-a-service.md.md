# Setting the Trial Period for a Service

#### FAQ - Setting the Trial Period for a Service in Agency Handy

1. \*\*What is the trial period feature in Agency Handy?\*\*
 \* The trial period feature allows potential clients to experience a service package for free before making a financial commitment.
2. \*\*What are the benefits of offering a trial period for a service?\*\*
 \* Benefits include attracting more clients, reducing the perceived risk for new users, and gathering valuable feedback to improve the service.
3. \*\*Who can set a trial period for a service in Agency Handy?\*\*
 \* Only users with an active Agency Handy account who are logged in can set up a trial period.
4. \*\*When can I set the trial period for a service?\*\*
 \* The trial period can be configured when creating or editing a service package.
5. \*\*How do I access the trial period setting in Agency Handy?\*\*
 \* Navigate to the service creation form after logging into your account. Click '+ Add More Packages' and locate the 'Trial Period' option.
6. \*\*How do I specify the trial duration?\*\*
 \* Click the 'Add Trial Period' button and enter the desired duration in days.
7. \*\*Can I change the trial duration after the service is published?\*\*
 \* Yes, you can edit the trial duration when updating the service package.
8. \*\*Can I offer different trial durations for multiple packages within a service?\*\*
 \* Yes, each package can have a unique trial duration set during creation or editing.
9. \*\*How does the trial period impact the client experience?\*\*
 \* Clients can use the service without paying upfront, allowing them to assess its value and suitability.
10. \*\*What should I consider when setting a trial duration?\*\*
 \* Choose a duration that balances giving clients enough time to explore while not overly extending the free period.
11. \*\*Can I remove the trial period after the service is active?\*\*
 \* Yes, you can remove or adjust the trial period by editing the service package.
12. \*\*What happens when the trial period ends?\*\*
 \* Clients will be prompted to subscribe or purchase the service at the standard rate.
13. \*\*Can I collect feedback during the trial period?\*\*
 \* Yes, using the trial period strategically can help gather client feedback to improve the service.
14. \*\*Will clients be notified when the trial period is about to end?\*\*
 \* It is recommended to set up automated notifications or communicate the end of the trial proactively.
15. \*\*Is the trial period feature available for both one-time and subscription services?\*\*
 \* Typically, trial periods are more commonly used with subscription services.
16. \*\*Can I specify terms and conditions for the trial period?\*\*
 \* Yes, it is advisable to clearly state any conditions or limitations in the service description.
17. \*\*Can a client cancel the trial period before it ends?\*\*
 \* Yes, clients should have the option to discontinue the trial if they choose.
18. \*\*Can I track how many clients are using the trial?\*\*
 \* Yes, Agency Handy provides insights into service usage, including trial periods.
19. \*\*What should I do if a client abuses the trial period?\*\*
 \* You can set limits on trial usage or revoke access if necessary, based on your terms.
20. \*\*Are there any legal considerations when offering a free trial?\*\*
 \* Make sure to include clear terms and conditions to avoid disputes or misuse.
21. \*\*How do I promote my service’s trial period effectively?\*\*
 \* Clearly highlight the trial period in your service description and marketing materials.
22. \*\*What if a client wants to extend the trial period?\*\*
 \* You can manually update the trial duration if necessary by editing the package settings.
