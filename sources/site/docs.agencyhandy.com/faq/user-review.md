**Agency Handy User Review Feature – Frequently Asked Questions**

---

**1\. What is the User Review feature in Agency Handy?**

The User Review feature allows clients to leave feedback on services they have purchased, including a 5-star rating and optional text feedback.

---

**2\. Who can leave a review on Agency Handy?**

Only clients who have purchased a service or completed at least one subscription cycle can leave a review.

---

**3\. Can a client leave multiple reviews for the same order?**

No, clients can leave only one review per order. However, they can update or resubmit their review if it gets rejected.

---

**4\. How can a client submit a review?**

Clients can log in, navigate to the **Orders** section, select the completed service, click on **Leave a Review**, and fill out the rating and feedback form.

---

**5\. What are the required fields when submitting a review?**

Clients must select a star rating (1 to 5). The feedback text is optional (up to 500 characters).

---

**6\. Can clients edit their review after submission?**

No, clients cannot edit a submitted review directly, but they can resubmit a new review if the original was rejected.

---

**7\. What happens if a review is rejected by the service provider?**

If a review is rejected, the client can resubmit an updated version without any limit on resubmissions.

---

**8\. How can a service provider request a review from a client?**

Service providers can go to the **Order** section, find the specific order, and click **Request Review**. They can also add a thank-you message when sending the request.

---

**9\. Can a client submit a star-only review without text?**

Yes, clients can choose to give just a star rating without any additional text.

---

**10\. Can service providers edit client reviews?**

No, providers can only approve, reject, or delete reviews but cannot edit the content.

---

**11\. Where can service providers manage submitted reviews?**

All submitted reviews can be managed in the **Review Management** section under the specific service.

---

**12\. Can a service provider delete an approved review?**

Yes, even after approval, a service provider can choose to delete a review if necessary.

---

**13\. How does a client know if their review was approved?**

The client will not receive a direct notification about approval, but the review will be visible on the service page once approved.

---

**14\. What happens if a client does not respond to a review request?**

Nothing happens. The client is not obligated to submit a review, even after receiving a request.

---

**15\. What if a client wants to change their review after approval?**

Clients cannot directly edit approved reviews. They need to contact the service provider to discuss resubmission.

---

**16\. How are reviews displayed on the service page?**

Approved reviews appear publicly, showing the average rating, breakdown of star ratings, and the latest 3-5 reviews with a **View All Reviews** option.

---

**17\. Can reviews be linked to specific packages within a service?**

No, reviews are linked to the entire service, not individual packages within it.

---

**18\. What kind of reviews can a client submit?**

Clients can submit a star rating with or without additional text feedback.

---

**19\. How can clients know if their review request is pending?**

Clients will receive a notification when an agency requests them to leave a review.

---

**20\. Can clients leave reviews for each subscription cycle?**

Yes, clients can leave a review after completing each subscription cycle.

---

**21\. What notifications do clients receive after submitting a review?**

After submission, clients receive a message saying: **"Thank you for submitting your review on \[Service Name\]."**

---

**22\. Can clients leave reviews from the client portal?**

Yes, clients can log into their portal, navigate to **Orders**, and leave a review from there.

---

**23\. How do service providers know when a new review is submitted?**

Service providers receive a notification stating: **"You’ve received a new review from \[Client Name\] on \[Service Name\]."**

---

**24\. Can clients leave reviews for services they haven’t purchased?**

No, only clients who have purchased a service can leave a review.

---

**25\. What should a client do if they encounter an error while submitting a review?**

Clients should refresh the page and try again or contact support for assistance.

---

**26\. Can service providers disable the review feature for specific services?**

No, the review feature is automatically available for all completed services.

---

**27\. How do service providers ensure the authenticity of a review?**

Only clients who have completed at least one service or subscription cycle can submit a review, reducing fake or irrelevant feedback.

---

**28\. How are ratings calculated for a service?**

The service page shows the average rating based on all approved reviews.

---

**29\. What should a service provider do if they receive inappropriate reviews?**

They can reject the review and, if necessary, delete it from the **Review Management** section.

---

**30\. Can clients update their review if they change their opinion?**

If a client’s initial review was rejected, they can submit a new one. Otherwise, they would need to contact the service provider for resubmission.
